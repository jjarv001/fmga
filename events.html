Throughout the year, our co-op hosts a variety of events, workshops, and classes. These events are designed to help people learn more about mushrooms, mushroom cultivation, and the benefits of mushrooms. We also host events to help people learn more about our co-op and the work we do. Check out our upcoming events below!

Event: Open House

Date: 3/14/25 - 3/16/25

Location: 1834 Twiggs Ferry Rd, Hartfield, VA 23071

Details:

Join us for our annual Open House event! This event is a great opportunity to learn more about our co-op, meet our members, and learn more about mushrooms. We will be hosting tours of our primary location, which is on the same property as the Chesapeake Bay Croquet Club. We will also have a variety of mushroom products available for purchase. This event is free and open to the public. That Sunday (3/16), we are also holding a workshop/class to teach people our cultivation methods. The cost is $40 per person, and the course is estimated to be 4-6 hours. Class size is limited so please reach out to one of our members if you're interested. We hope to see you there!
