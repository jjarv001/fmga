Resources

In this section you'll find much of the information that we teach in our in-person courses. Much of this material is still being developed/refined, and is very much a work-in-progress. We're always open to suggestions/feedback so feel free to contact us with any ideas you may have.

Also keep in mind that while this information covers much of what is covered in our in-person courses, it is not a substitute for hands-on experience.

While this information is openly available for personal use and educational purposes, it is not to be used for commercial purposes without explicit permission from the Forest Mushroom Growers Association.

Finally, while this information is provided for free, it is not free to produce. Much time and effort goes into the production of this material, so we ask that if you find this information useful, please consider taking one of our in-person courses and/or looking into our line of products. Thank you!
