About Us

We are a group of mushroom producers residing in the Middle Peninsula of Virginia. The vast majority of our products come from mushrooms grown from logs and stumps. We are a small group of people who are passionate about mushrooms and want to share our knowledge with others. Mushrooms (and the mycelium beneath) are a vital part of the ecosystem. Luckily for us, they are also delicious and nutritious! Our goal is to provide a nutritious, delicious, and planet-friendly product to our customers. We also encourage and educate our customers in methods of mushroom cultivation. As a co-op, we work to develop training materials, instructions/tutorials, and recipes for our customers to use on their own. FMGA is a group of people who are passionate about the outdoors, our fungi friends, and the future of our planet. Please join us in our mission to "Inoculate the World"!
